Cooking is an activity in Rune Factory 4. To begin cooking, the protagonist must speak to Porcoline to obtain a cooking table for free. After this, he or she must order an "EZ Cooking License." Upon passing, Porcoline will sell a knife, frying pan, and mixer. After ordering the "Pro Cooking License," Porcoline will sell an oven, steamer, and a pot.

Cooking basically is similar to other Harvest Moon and Rune Factory games. The Player must have the correct 'Tool' or 'Utensil' as well as any Required Ingredients in order to make any Cooked Dish successfully. Cooking Bread can be purchased from Porcoline for 100G. It will contain one or more Recipes but if your Cooking Level is too low, your Character will declare that he/she 'learned nothing'. The Cooking Bread therefore will be wasted!
